Phishing Attack Awareness Simulator

This page helps you recognize a phishing email by letting you inspect a simulated message, identify red flags, and decide whether to report it.

Instructions:
  • Read the simulated email carefully.
  • Click "Inspect email" to see the message details.
  • Choose whether to "Report as phishing" or "Ignore / Mark safe".
  • Review the warning message to learn the important signs.

From: support@secure-bank-alerts.com

Subject: Urgent: Verify your account now

Dear customer,

We detected unauthorized access to your account. Click the link below to verify your identity and restore access immediately.

Verify your account

Please respond within 24 hours or your account will be suspended.

Thank you,
Secure Bank Alerts Team

Phishing red flags:
  • Sender address does not match the official bank domain.
  • Urgent language designed to create panic.
  • Generic greeting like "Dear customer".
  • Links that do not clearly show the real destination.
